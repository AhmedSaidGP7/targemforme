Drafting and translation of authorizations

Complete your contract safely!

We help you to draft, translate and authenticate your necessary authorizations.

Nationality authorizations

We help you by explaining to you the steps you need to take in signing the authorization to obtain Turkish nationality, and in letting you know and translating for you the necessary documents needed. We equally help you to complete authorizations for obtaining residency permit.

Buying and selling authorizations

Translate4Me helps you to obtain suitable and required drafts for property buying and selling authorizations. It also helps you on how to complete the authorization process.

Schools and university authorization

Translate4Me helps students to accomplish authorizations relating to university registration, equation of academic certificates and obtainment of residency permits.

Commercial authorizations

    We help you to complete authorizations relating to establishment of companies or buying or selling shares in companies. We also help you on tax exception authorizations, property and car renting authorizations and other authorizations.
