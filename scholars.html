All Academic Materials You Need.

Are you writing an academic research?

We can help you prepare for your academic research to reach the level you desire by supplying you with important and rare resources that can really enable you write a successful dissertation.

Providing scientific materials to researchers

Researchers, especially in the Arab world, need academic materials to enrich their researches and give them added value. Towards this end, Translate4Me enables researchers at different levels to obtain these essential materials, especially in the field of social sciences. It also helps them translate these materials with highest degree of accuracy and professionalism.

Supplying Historical Documents

World archives are full of huge and rich documents about the Arab world. Of these documents are British, Turkish, French, Russian and others. Translate4Me takes it upon itself the task of helping teachers and researchers to benefit from these documents and others by providing them and translating them into Arabic in an honest and accurate manner. Tranlate4Me also aims at establishing cooperation partnership with Arab archives and libraries in this regard.

Providing Rare Sources

World libraries are full of extremely important and rich sources about the Arab world and Arab countries, cultures and history. Some of these very important sources are old and rare magazines and newspapers. Translate4Me enables the Arab researcher as well as Arab libraries and universities to access these sources, overcome language barriers and fully benefit from these sources.

Consultancy in areas of research and writing

    Translate4Me provides consultancy whereby it enables researchers to access sources and references they need, especially Turkish archives and libraries, and how to get them, deal with them and make the best use of them.
